Crypto-Viewer

Mobile app to track your assets

Project

Crypto-Viewer is a mobile application to fetch cryptos, get crypto analysis, and manage your assets via a protfolio.
GitHub
Play Store
Dribbble
React Native
Expo
Typescript

Features

Fetch prices

For each crypto, fetch current buy and sell prices, as well as volume and high and low values

Fetch historical values

See historical values, for one day, one week, one month, or one year frequency

Add to your wallet

Once added to your wallet, follow your total and each crypto statistics
