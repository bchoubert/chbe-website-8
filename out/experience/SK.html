Maplink

Maplink is both a map component integrated into DICT.fr and a Network and Urban Properties Management Solution
Based on GeoServer, MapLink is a product used internally, externally, and as part of another product DICT.fr. DICT.fr offers construction work declaration, which is mandatory in France. Used externally or internally, Maplink permits companies to manage their networks, update them as build was done.
